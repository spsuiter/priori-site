Core Capabilities
Everything from IFW pull to signed response.

Priori handles the full prosecution workflow — on your server, in your network, under your control.

📥

IFW Collector

Pulls the complete USPTO file history (IFW) directly from ODP. No manual downloading. Parses OA rejections automatically.

✍️

OA Response Generator

Generates a full draft Office Action response informed by examiner analytics, firm argument history, and the Firm Library reference corpus.

🧠

Prosecution Intelligence

Harvests your firm's complete prosecution history and computes examiner-level analytics: allow rates, average OA counts, CPC specialties, and pendency percentiles.

Patent Pending
🎯

Claim Scope Tuner

Systematically optimizes claim scope across six axes — §101, §102/103, §112, detectability, design-around resistance, and invalidity exposure.

Patent Pending
🔍

Quality Review (OCQR)

Screens claims and specification for patent porn, antecedent basis errors, §112(f) nonce words, reference numeral gaps, and described-not-claimed issues.

Patent Pending
👩‍💼

Inventor Review

Translates claims into plain language, cross-validates against inventor disclosures, and generates a structured sign-off checklist for inventor review meetings.

Patent Pending
📚

Corpus Manager

Stores approved OA→Response pairs as few-shot examples. The AI learns your firm's drafting style, argument strategies, and amendment patterns.

Patent Pending
📋

Patent App Generator

Drafts full patent applications from inventor disclosures, including claims, detailed description, and abstract. Art unit analytics inform drafting strategy.

📑

IDS Generator (SB/08b)

Drop in NPL PDFs and get formatted IDS citation tables ready for filing. Supports US patents, foreign references, and non-patent literature.
